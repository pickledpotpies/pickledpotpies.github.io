Our Goal
As the #1 toilet seller in the nation, our goal is to improve the toilet experience for our consumers. The average person spends about 813.3 days on the toilet throughout their life. We want to ensure that your time on the lavatory is as comfortable as possible, for the lowest price possible. Our company provides a wide variety of toilets, urinals, and bidets. At Flush Factory, we use the highest quality porcelain in each of our models. We have also designed the most innovative and efficient flush, conserving our planet's water supply.
Founding Flush
Flush Factory Inc was founded all the way back in 1985. Our CEO, Poe Celin began Flush Factory after being left unsatisfied with the quality of the toilets used in public spaces. He sketched out the first design for the TopFlush® and got to work. Once the design was made, he began searching for investors to build the first TopFlush®. After securing a $50,000 investment, the first ever TopFlush® began production. TopFlushes began being installed in homes, malls, schools, workplaces and restaurants all over America. With the TopFlush® Model One, Flush Factory rose to the #1 toilet seller of America in 1988. Since then, Flush Factory has continued to innovate how toilets are made with our new TopFlush® Model Four.

CEO Poe Celin

Poe Celin, born in 1963 in Waterbury, CT, founded Flush Factory in 1985. Inspired by his desire for improvement, he created the TopFlush®, revolutionizing toilet design. Thanks to his determination and innovation, Flush Factory has become the leader in bathroom technology.
